--- How To Use ---
1. Install Bank Gothic Font ("bank gothic.ttf") before opening project
2. Open Template with After Effects
3. Change Text in TITLE composition (green) and PRESENTS layer (green) as necassary
4. Render

--- More Information ---
In case Optical Flares or CC Particle World are not installed:
Deactivate VFX layers (purple), activate video layer (orange) instead

Activate Sound Output in Render Options, otherwise you will hear nothing ;)

--- Have fun! ---
